FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Khaata free to get started?
+
Yes, our Starter plan is completely free forever. You can manage up to 100 invoices and 5 customers without adding a credit card.
Does it support all GST tax rates in India?
+
Absolutely. We natively support the 0%, 3%, 5%, 18%, and 28% GST slabs. Tax is computed automatically as you create invoices.
How do I import my existing invoices?
+
We provide a powerful seed script to load your historical data in bulk, or you can use our simple CSV importer from the dashboard settings.
Can I manage multiple customers at once?
+
Yes, Khaata includes a built-in CRM. Every invoice is tied to a customer profile, so you can track total outstanding balances per client.
Is the data stored securely?
+
Security is our priority. Your financial data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.
What happens if an invoice goes overdue?
+
Overdue invoices are automatically highlighted in red on your dashboard and summary analytics, ensuring you never miss a follow-up.
